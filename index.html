Personal

I'm passionate about empowering people to reach their full potential.

Following dreams is a privilege, and I want to use my privilege to the fullest. Because of this, I live by a few guiding principles. Never settle. Always have something bigger and better to work towards, some new idea for how to contribute something meaningful to the world. Only work on things that energize you. Don't take life too seriously. There's too much living and laughing to do for everything to get your attention. Save your energy for what matters and invest it into accomplishing amazing things. Be the person who inspires you. Not only be your own inspiration, but also don't dream of being your role models: be your role models. Let inspirations drive you to become better. Work on what you love. If you're not working on what you love, then you're cutting yourself short. You're spending over 30% of your day doing what you don't enjoy, and that's not worth it. Your work should be your life's work. Surround yourself with awesome. That means awesome everything. Awesome things, awesome hobbies, an awesome job, and most importantly, awesome people. Get rid of the things that don't make you happy and spend your time with things that do. They say you're the average of the 5 people you spend the most time with, and I believe that about everything else too. Surround yourself with things that help you grow. Surround yourself with things that make you awesome.
