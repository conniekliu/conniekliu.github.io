👋

I'm Connie, an entrepreneur passionate about empowering people.

I'm an engineer turned educator turned entrepreneur & coach. Most recently, I was the founding Executive Director of Project Invent and a Stanford MBA student. Now, I'm working on a new venture to bring coaching to everyone.

engineer

From a young age, I loved building things. I started with building mousetrap vehicles. I upgraded to building electric go-karts and assistive technologies like FingerReader and Penpal.

educator

By engineering impactful products, I felt empowered in an unfamiliar way. Why didn't I get this experience earlier? I became passionate about changing how and what we teach in classrooms. In 2016, I became a teacher.

entrepreneur

In 2018, I spun out a class of mine into Project Invent, a national nonprofit bringing opportunities to innovate to everyone. Fast forward to today, and we've empowered 1000+ students to invent solutions to real problems in their communities and become the changemakers we need.

Now I'm building something new: stay tuned!
