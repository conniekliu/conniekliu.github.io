Join the Table

The Problem

How can we help teachers adopt Harkness methods in their own classroom?

The Solution

Create an online platform to give teachers the information and support they need.

Join the Table is an online platform to help teachers transform their traditional classroom into a Harkness one. It gives teachers the information they need to get started and the supportive network of other teachers and experts to keep them going. You can explore the website concept here.

Previous to developing this platform, I conducted both quantitative and qualitative research to identify the core components of the Harkness classroom. Using data from 242 student respondents in grades 9-12 at Phillips Exeter Academy from the 2015-2016 academic year, I analyzed the student evaluations of the Harkness model. This involved identifying the key components of Harkness through qualitative analysis, identifying how Harkness practices differed for different subjects through scale models and linear regression models, and performing classroom video analysis to determine core features of Harkness classroom etiquette.

The research helped inform the key ideas to emphasize in the Join the Table platform to help teachers bring Harkness to their own classrooms. By identifying the core components, these could be included on Join the Table as tangible goals for teachers to achieve in their classrooms. With the forum feature, teachers would be able to troubleshoot problems and share information with other teachers who are similarly starting Harkness classrooms. Hired experts would also frequent the forum to answer any outstanding questions and help teachers along the way. In the future, Join the Table could also be extended as a platform for sharing and piloting many education models.
