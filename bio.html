Connie is an engineer turned teacher turned entrepreneur & coach, passionate about empowering people of all ages. Recognized by Forbes 30 Under 30 for her work, the program has launched in 100+ schools in 25 states. Connie received her MBA from Stanford and studied mechanical engineering at MIT. She is now building a coaching startup, and is an executive coach and cat mom on the side.
