    • ARTBOT: A ROBOTIC ARTIST FOR KIDS

    An Introduction to Robotics

    The ArtBot is a low-cost, easy-to-make robot for students of all ages as an easy introduction to robotics. Using a paper cup, three markers, a vibrating motor, and batteries, the ArtBot can walk along a page on its marker legs and create an "artpiece". By offsetting the battery weight on the top of the cup, students can make the ArtBot walk in various patterns. Students experimented with different weight distributions, motors, and number of markers to make different pictures. Over 200 student visitors built an ArtBot at the 2013 Cambridge Science Fair and we intend to use this design for many more outreach events in the future.
