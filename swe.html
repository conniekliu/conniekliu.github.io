MIT Society of Women Engineers Outreach

The Problem

How can we inspire young girls with STEAM education?

The Solution

Provide numerous opportunities for young girls to have unique STEM experiences.

MIT SWE Outreach is dedicated to inspiring young girls to be interested in STEM. By running unique programs to target all K-12 age groups, MIT SWE . Serving over 2000 young girls from all over the globe every year, MIT SWE strives to help repair the leaky STEM pipeline and encourage more girls to stay in STEM.

My Role

I served as Vice President of Outreach and as various outreach chairs throughout my undergraduate career. During this time, I started seven new outreach programs. From connecting collegebound high school girls worldwide to MIT mentors through College Connection to empowering underprivileged middle school students with the creativity of design thinking through Design Squad, my programs targeted students from all backgrounds to be inspired by STEM.
