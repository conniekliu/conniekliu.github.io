Personal

I'm passionate about empowering people to reach their full potential.

Following dreams is a privilege, and I want to use my privilege to the fullest. Because of this, I live by a few guiding principles. Never settle. Never settle for less than perfect. Always have something bigger and better to work towards, some new idea for how to contribute something meaningful to the world. Only work on things that energize you. Be the person who inspires you. Not only be your own inspiration, but also don't dream of being your role models: be your role models. Let inspirations drive you to become better. Work and life are the same thing. If you're not working on what you love, then you're cutting yourself short. You're spending over 30% of your day doing things you don't enjoy, and that's not worth it. Your work should be your life's work. Surround yourself with awesome. That means awesome everything. Awesome things, awesome hobbies, an awesome job, and most importantly, awesome people. Get rid of the things in your life that don't make you happy and spend your time with things that do. They say you're the average of the 5 people you spend the most time with, and I believe that about everything else too. Surround yourself with things that help you grow. Surround yourself with things that make you awesome.
