PenPal

The Problem

How can we help to restore the independence of the millions of people living with tremors?

The Solution

A tremor canceling pen that stabilizes tremors to help people write independently again.

Penpal is the first tremor canceling pen that combines software and hardware to make a smart solution to the widely experienced problem of writing tremors. It uses active stabilization to counteract tremors much more effectively than solutions currently on the market. Inside, a gimbal mechanism and a stabilization control algorithm work in tandem to stabilize the pen. The outer shell isolates your tremors from the internal pen to make your handwriting tremor-free.

PenPal is also adaptable. It isn't just a pen, but can also be used as a stylus, pencil, paintbrush, and more. By replacing the insert with any writing utensil, all kinds of activities can benefit from PenPal's unique stabilizing technologies.

Finally, Penpal comes packaged with a web application that allows users to keep track of their own tremor amplitudes and frequencies, providing important information about users' muscle developments.

Lessons Learned

Designed and programmed a gimbaled Segway-like stabilization mechanism using Arduino, 3D printing, and machining.

Collaborated with a computer scientist to integrate software and users to factor in user feedback.

Designed on a strict budget to make the product affordable yet still intuitive to use and effective in function.

Created novel solutions for distinguishing intentional writing movements from tremor movements.
